Save The Dogs

Introduction

Animals are getting abused everyday. They are being chained to the floor, left to starve; and even getting skinned for their fur. Over 1 million of these animals are dogs. Many of these dogs get abused because of domestic violence that is going on where they and their owners live.. These poor dogs don't have a voice to stand up for themselves; that is why we need to do something for them. Dogs can serve as companions and serving disable and the eldery, but instead they are being treated cruel by many. Los Angeles has one of the biggest dog abuse cases in the United States. In summer of 2019 a 18 year old boy was charged with second-degree cruelty to animals. In this project you will learn all about dog abuse and cruelty and learn how you can help end it.
